Re-discovering consumer needs and identifying missing aspects in modern diet and lifestyle disease management solutions, InstaEats has developed an all natural range of health management nutritive solutions through the finest selection of most active and potent superfoods delivering targeted health solution, convenience of consumption and fulfillment of taste and healthy snacking.

Rekha is an award winning dietitian, women entrepreneur and the mind behind InstaEats. Her dynamic experience that spans over a decade with an in-depth understanding of Indian dietary profile, diet practices, lifestyle habits and diseases acquired over thousands of client interactions, health care summits, workshops and research studies has been the driving force and inspiration behind InstaEats.

Building an India that stays ahead of diabetes, PCOS, thyroid and heart diseases and where kids, adults and the elderly stay fit, healthy and active.
