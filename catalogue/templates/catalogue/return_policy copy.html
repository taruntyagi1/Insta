Returns & Refund Policy

Thanks for purchasing our products at InstaEats.

Due to the nature of our business and products we sell, items that expire sooner than 2 months from the date of the purchase are not eligible for a refund.

For any other items to be eligible for a refund, you have to return the item you have purchased to us within 7 calendar days of the purchase. The item must be unopened and in its original condition.

Refund will also be provided in case:

  • Customer does not get the delivery of the ordered products within 30 days if InstaEats fails to ship the ordered products.
  • The shipping location is not serviced by our partner courier companies.
  • If our products arrive to you damaged, rotten or contaminated in any way, please contact us right away and we will be happy to send a free replacement regardless of its expiration date.

No Returns will be entertained if a customer wants to return the product for the reason that he/she doesn't like it after delivery of the product or feels the product doesn't match his or her expectations.

No refunds will be given in the following cases:

  • Incorrect or insufficient address mentioned by the customer
  • Non- availability of recipient at the mentioned address and/or premises
  • Refusal to accept products
  • Delivered at the place/to the person specifically mentioned by the customer other than the customer himself.
  • Force majeure event
  • In case the product has undergone any tampering by the customer

If anything is unclear or you have more questions feel free to contact our customer support team. You can reach out to us at orders@instaeats.in
